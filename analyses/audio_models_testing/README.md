This is the files for testing the 2 audio models we have selected - Emotion2vec_base and Way2vec

These audio models are tested on the RAVDEES dataset from Kaggle, a comprehensive dataset with audio and visual recordings and emotion tags.

Through our testing, we identify the Accuracy, Macro and Weighted F1 score of our tested model on the RAVDEES dataset. Outputs also include a confusion matrix showing the performance of model on RAVDEES dataset.
